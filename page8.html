My work history!

Jeffery J. Jeraldson's work history is as diverse as it is impressive, weaving together a tapestry of creative pursuits, technical mastery, and entrepreneurial ventures that showcase his multifaceted talents and unwavering determination.

Professional Berimbau Player (2005-2015)

Jeffery began his career on the dunes, where the haunting melodies of the berimbau called to him. Over a decade, he transformed this calling into a profession, touring extensively and performing at a variety of venues, from intimate desert gatherings to grand, international stages. His virtuoso skills not only earned him acclaim but also deepened his connection to his cultural roots and the broader community of desert dwellers.

Lead Developer, OasisTech (2016-2018)

Leveraging his self-taught expertise in programming, Jeffery transitioned into the tech world as a lead developer for OasisTech, a startup focused on creating innovative water conservation technologies. Here, he utilized his proficiency in multiple programming languages to develop software solutions that helped communities across the desert to efficiently manage and conserve water resources.

Founder and CEO, THE FROG STORE (2018-Present)

Inspired by his desire to unite and uplift the amphibian community, Jeffery embarked on his most ambitious venture yet: THE FROG STORE. As the founder and CEO, he utilized his doctorate in business and technological acumen to create a thriving online marketplace. This platform not only serves as a conduit for frog-made products but also champions the entrepreneurial spirit of frogs everywhere, transforming the way they connect, create, and commerce.

Consultant and Speaker (2019-Present)

Recognizing the value of his journey and expertise, Jeffery also dedicates time to consulting for startups and speaking at conferences. His insights into overcoming adversity, innovative business strategies, and the intersection of technology and sustainability have made him a sought-after voice in both the entrepreneurial and environmental spheres.

Jeffery J. Jeraldson's work history reflects a journey marked by creativity, innovation, and a deep commitment to community. From the rhythmic beats of the berimbau to the digital marketplace, his career is a testament to the power of passion, perseverance, and the pursuit of a vision that transcends the ordinary.
